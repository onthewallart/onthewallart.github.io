The Artist

About Susan

Susan Graham paints the garden as she sees it — loose, luminous, and full of life. Working in watercolour and acrylic from her home in Dorchester, Ontario, she lets the colour pool and bloom across the surface, catching flowers at their most alive: poppies mid-sway, a magnolia just opening, a bird of paradise reaching for the light. Her brush roams freely, too — to quiet landscapes and shorelines, birds and wildlife, figures, and the occasional bold abstract.

Every piece is painted by hand, one at a time. Her hope is a simple one — that a little of that colour and quiet finds its way onto your wall, and brings the garden indoors all year round. Some pieces are available as originals and others have already found their homes; if something catches your eye, reach out and Susan will let you know what's available.

— Susan Graham · Dorchester, Ontario
