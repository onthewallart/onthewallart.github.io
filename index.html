The Artist

About Susan

Susan Graham paints the garden as she sees it — loose, luminous, and full of life. Working in watercolour from her home in Dorchester, Ontario, she lets the colour pool and bloom across the paper, catching flowers at their most alive: poppies mid-sway, a magnolia just opening, a bird of paradise reaching for the light.

Every piece is painted by hand, one at a time. Her hope is a simple one — that a little of that colour and quiet finds its way onto your wall, and brings the garden indoors all year round.

— Susan Graham · Dorchester, Ontario
